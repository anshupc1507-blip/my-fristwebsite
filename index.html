Institute Technical Council (ITC) at IIT Bombay is at the forefront of nurturing student innovation, technical skills
leadership. We provide students with the resources and guidance needed to bring their ideas to life through a wide range of technical clubs, teams, and events.
With a focus on fostering collaboration and creativity, ITC plays a pivotal role in organizing technical workshops, competitions, and
projects. From aeromodelling to artificial intelligence, the council caters to a diverse set of technical interests, making it a hub for student-led innovation.

about us

ITC's mission is to empower students to explore and excel in the world of technology, fostering a culture of innovation and excellence at IIT Bombay.
We are committed to providing a platform for students to develop their technical skills, collaborate on projects, and contribute to the vibrant technical community on campus.
Through our initiatives, we aim to inspire the next generation of technologists and leaders, driving positive change through technology.
Join us in our journey to create a dynamic and inclusive technical ecosystem at IIT Bombay!
